Ground Pork Tacos with Pineapple Salsa.

Easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 1 tbsp soy-based liquid seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon chipotle powder
  • 1 clove garlic

Pineapple Salsa

  • 1 cup diced fresh pineapple
  • 1/4 cup minced red onion
  • 1 small jalapeno pepper(seeded and minced)
  • 2 tbsp minced fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 lime juice
  • salt
  • 8(6 inch) corn tortillas

Directions

Step 1

  • Combine ground pork, liquid seasoning, ground chipotlepowder, and garlic in bowl.

    Set aside for flavors to meld.

Step 2

Combine pineapple, red onion, jalapeno, cilantro, lime juice, and salt in a bowl.

Set pineapple salsa aside.

Step 3

Preheat a large skillet over high heat.

Crumble groung pork into the hot skillet.

Cook and stir until pork is completely browned and edges begin to crisp, 7 to 10 minutes.

Drain any excess grease.

Place pork in a bowl and keep warm.

Wipe out skillet.

Step 4

Warm tortillas in skillet over medium-low heat, about 20 seconds per side.

Divide meat mixture between tortillas and top with pineapple salsa
